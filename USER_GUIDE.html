üîß Troubleshooting & FAQ

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

üîê Why can't I log in to my account?

Solutions:

  • Check your internet connection
  • Verify email and password are correct
  • Try resetting your password
  • Clear app cache and restart
  • Ensure Firebase services are accessible in your region
üîÑ Google Fit sync is not working

Solutions:

  • Check internet connection
  • Verify Google Fit app is installed and updated
  • Re-authenticate Google account in app settings
  • Grant all required permissions
  • Try manual sync from Connection Status screen
üîä Sound effects are not playing

Solutions:

  • Check device volume settings
  • Enable sounds in app settings
  • Restart the app
  • Check if Do Not Disturb mode is enabled
  • Verify app has audio permissions
‚åö Wearable device won't connect

Solutions:

  • Ensure Bluetooth is enabled on both devices
  • Check if devices are within range (10 meters)
  • Install required companion apps (Samsung Health, Wear OS)
  • Grant Bluetooth permissions to the app
  • Restart both devices and try reconnecting
üìä My health score seems incorrect

Explanation:

  • Health score is calculated based on goal completion percentages
  • Steps: 30% of total score
  • Water: 30% of total score
  • Sleep: 40% of total score (duration + quality)
  • Check if your goals are realistic and properly set
  • Ensure all data is logged correctly for the day
